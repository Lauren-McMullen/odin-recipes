Greek Yogurt Pancakes

Recipe Credit: yellowblissroad.com

A new way to pancake!

For many of us, a good pancake breakfast envokes the dreamt nostalgia of weekend mornings and summer vacation. Luckily, pancakes can also be a cost effective and delicious dinner for students. These protein-packed greek yogurt pancakes use only 8 common ingredients and take less than 30 minutes to whip up. Add chocolate chips, blueberries, or any other mix-in you want for an extra falvour punch!

What you'll need (ingredients)

  • 1 1/2 cups flour
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 2 tbsp white sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 3/4 cup plain greek yogurt
  • 2/3 cup milk

Directions

  1. Preheat a large griddle or frying pan to low-medium heat.
  2. In a large bowl whisk together the flour, baking powder, baking soda, salt & sugar.
  3. In a separate bowl whisk the eggs. Then whisk in the yogurt and milk.
  4. Gently fold the wet ingredients into the flour mixture using a rubber spatula or wooden spoon. The batter will be a little lumpy.
  5. Lightly grease your frying pan/griddle with oil or non-stick cooking spray.
  6. For each pancake, pour Â¼ cup of batter onto the griddle. Fry for 2-3 minutes, or until you see a few air bubbles on the uncooked side. Flip and continue to fry for 2-3 minutes on the second side or until golden.
  7. Repeat with the rest of the batter.
  8. Serve warm with fruit, syrup, whipped cream or whatever toppings you prefer.
